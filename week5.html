Ball Kirby Bouncing

For this week, I have chosen to a ball bouncing as my animation piece. When I had first selected this piece, I wanted to do something different. As I thought of things that were round, I had thought of Kirby! As such, this piece has Ball Kirby from Kirby: Canvas Curse as the main object being animated. To make the animation more interesting, on top of the ball bouncing off the walls, I had decided to make the ball also roll once it hits the ground.
