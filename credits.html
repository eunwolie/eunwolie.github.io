Programs/Languages Used to Make the Mosaic:

  • Adobe Illustrator - To sketch the outlines of the Eevee and to obtain SVG coordinates for the shape.
  • Audacity - To manipulate the downloaded music to obtain 30 seconds samples clips
  • HTML, CSS and Javascript - To ensure the mosaic is interactive with the webpage
  • Rapidtables.com - To help with the selection of apropriate shades of brown and cream colour tones

Music List:

Original Pokemon Yellow/Red/Blue Soundtrack by Nintendo Pokemon Let's Go Pikachu & Eevee Soundtrack by Nintendo
