Pokemon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire

Pokemon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire is a remake of third generation Pokemon games Ruby and Sapphire. Released in November 21, 2014 in North America, the remake contains game mechanics that were introduced in the subsequent generations of Pokemon games in addition to several different aesthetics and plot additions to the original versions of the game. As with many of the previous generation of Pokemon games, two versions of the game have been released, each version containing a special subset of Pokemon specific to that version.

Gyms

A big part of the game mechanics in any Pokemon game is to collect Pokemon and train them up in order to defeat gym leaders. Each gym leader is associated to an element type in the game. By defeating these gym leaders, players are able to progress further in the game. This is no different in Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire.
